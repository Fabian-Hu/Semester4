#### c)

$$
U = R * I
$$
$$
P = U * I
$$
$$
P = R * I * I
$$
$$
I^2 = \frac{P}{R}
$$
$$
I = \sqrt{\frac{P}{R}}
$$
$$
I = \sqrt{\frac{0,33W}{1000000 \Omega }} = 0.000574456264654A = 574 \mu A
$$
